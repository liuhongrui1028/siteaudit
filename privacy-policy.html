Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

1. Overview

SiteAudit ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our browser extension.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

SiteAudit is designed with privacy-first principles. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address, phone number)
  • Browsing history or visited URLs
  • Webpage content or HTML data
  • Login credentials or passwords
  • Financial or payment information
  • Health information
  • Location data

3. How SiteAudit Works

All scanning operations are performed locally within your browser. When you initiate a scan, the extension analyzes the currently open webpage using built-in libraries (including axe-core for accessibility testing). No data is sent to external servers.

4. Local Storage

SiteAudit stores the following information locally on your device using Chrome's storage API:

  • Daily scan count (to enforce free-tier limits)
  • Pro activation status (if you upgrade)

This data never leaves your device and is cleared if you uninstall the extension.

5. Third-Party Services

SiteAudit integrates the following third-party libraries:

  • axe-core by Deque Systems, Inc. (MPL-2.0 license) — used for accessibility scanning
  • html2pdf.js (MIT license) — used for PDF report generation

These libraries run entirely within your browser and do not transmit data to their respective developers.

6. Payments

If you purchase SiteAudit Pro, payment processing is handled by Gumroad (or your chosen payment processor). We do not collect or store your payment information. Please refer to Gumroad's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle payment data.

7. Children's Privacy

SiteAudit is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
