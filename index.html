Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the accessibility scan?

We use axe-core, the industry-standard engine from Deque (also used by Google Lighthouse and Microsoft Accessibility Insights). Automated scans catch approximately 30% of WCAG issues — perfect for a fast first pass, but not a replacement for manual testing or legal counsel.

Is my data safe?

100%. All scanning happens locally inside your browser. We never see your URLs, HTML, or scan results. No tracking, no analytics, no remote servers.

Can I cancel Pro anytime?

Yes. There are no contracts, no setup fees, and no cancellation penalties. You control your subscription through Gumroad.

Who is SiteAudit for?

Freelance developers, digital agencies, small business owners, and anyone who manages a website and needs fast, affordable compliance insights.

Does this protect me from ADA lawsuits?

SiteAudit helps you identify and fix common accessibility issues. However, it does not guarantee legal compliance or replace a professional manual audit. We recommend using it as a screening tool, then consulting an accessibility expert for high-risk sites.
